Why should I clean between my teeth?

It’s important to clean the gap between your teeth, which makes up 40% of the tooth surface but is responsible for about 80% of major dental problems. Proper cleaning reduces the risk of decay, gum disease, and serious gum and bone issues.

Piksters® clean the areas between your teeth where toothbrushes cannot reach

How often should I use Piksters?

Breathe Right Use Piksters® at least once a day when cleaning your teeth. It can also be used with fluoride toothpaste for better decay prevention in the gaps.

Piksters® can be carried in your pocket and used in many situations, particularly after meals when you can’t access a bathroom.
